A great flyer grabs attention in under three seconds. Start with a single bold headline that answers what's in it for the reader — leave the fine print for later. Keep your layout clean: one dominant image, one clear call-to-action, and no more than three typefaces. For print, always work in CMYK at 300 DPI and include a 3 mm bleed on all sides. Dark colours look richer on our 350 gsm silk stock — avoid very light greys, which can disappear in the finished print.
